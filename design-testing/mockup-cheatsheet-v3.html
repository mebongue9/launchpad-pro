The 3 Money-Making Reel Scripts

REEL #1: The Problem-Solution Reel
Hook Templates
  • "If you have [X followers], you should be making $[X] every day"
  • "Here's why you're not making money with [X] followers"
  • "I make $2,500 per reel — here's what I say"
Script Structure
  1. Hook (0-3 seconds): State the problem directly
  2. Agitate (3-6 seconds): Why it's costing them money
  3. Solution (6-12 seconds): Your digital product solves this
  4. Clear CTA: "Link in bio" or "Comment [WORD] below"
Example Script
"If you have 100 followers, you should be making $50-100 daily. Most people aren't because they don't know what to sell. I created a $27 course that shows you exactly how to turn followers into buyers. Link in bio."
Caption Formula
  • Line 1: Restate the hook
  • Lines 2-4: Share your experience/results
  • Line 5: Clear call-to-action
  • Use 2,200 characters max

REEL #2: The Transformation Reel
Before/After Templates
  • "6 months ago vs today"
  • "When I started vs now"
  • "My first month vs this month"
Script Structure
  1. Before State: Where you/client started
  2. After State: Current results
  3. The Bridge: Your product/method
  4. Proof: Specific numbers
Example Script
"6 months ago: $0 from Instagram. Today: $20,000/month. The difference? I started selling one $97 digital product. Same strategy I teach inside my course. Comment TRANSFORM to get it."
Visual Elements
  • Use text overlays for numbers
  • Show screenshots of earnings
  • Keep clips 0.6 seconds each
  • Total reel: 2-3 seconds

REEL #3: The Authority Reel
Authority Hooks
  • "I made $300K last month doing this"
  • "Here's how to build a million-dollar business with 1 hour/day"
  • "I get 70,000 new followers monthly — here's how"
Script Structure
  1. Credibility Statement: Your biggest result
  2. Method Preview: Quick overview
  3. Benefit Stack: What they get
  4. Urgency/Scarcity: Why now
Example Script
"I make $20,000/day selling digital products. 80% of your time should drive traffic to checkout pages. I created 40 viral reels in 2 hours. This system is inside my $47 blueprint. Comment BLUEPRINT below."
Posting Strategy
  • Post at 7am, 10am, 1pm in your timezone
  • Repost your best performers exactly
  • Use talking head style for authority
  • Include clear pricing in the pitch
