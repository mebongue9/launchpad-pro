Chapter 3: Converting Views Into Daily Sales
The Math:
  • 100 views → 5 profile visits → 1 link click → $50 sale
  • 1,000 views → 50 profile visits → 10 link clicks → $300-600
  • Scale: More reels = more views = more sales
  • --
Link in Bio Strategy:
  • Direct to checkout (not landing page)
  • One product only (no confusion)
  • Price clearly visible ($27, $47, $97)
  • UseProof installed (social proof pop-ups)
Pricing Sweet Spots:
  • $27: Impulse buy threshold
  • $47: Perceived value increase
  • $97: Higher commitment, better buyers
  • Avoid: $37, $67, $87 (psychological dead zones)
  • --
Story Pitching Rotation:
  • Day 1: Behind-scenes of success
  • Day 2: Student testimonial share
  • Day 3: "Link in bio if you want this"
  • Day 4: Process/method tease
  • Day 5: Urgency/scarcity element
  • Repeat cycle - never same approach daily

Chapter 4: Traffic Multiplication Hacks

Chapter 4: Traffic Multiplication Hacks
Double Hook Strategy
  • Hook #1: First 2 seconds grab attention
  • Hook #2: Seconds 3-4 create curiosity
  • Example: "Here's how to get 3-6 sales..." + "...for every 100 views"
  • Works because it creates compound interest
The 2-Day Similar Reel Hack
  • Publish similar reel 2 days after original
  • Original reel often explodes after similar one posts
  • Change 1 small detail only
  • Keep same hook structure
  • Same posting time
Caption Edit Revival Method
  • Edit your reel caption twice (not once)
  • Wait 24 hours between edits
  • Can revive dying reels
  • Spikes views unexpectedly
  • No explanation why it works - just does
Best Reel Reposting Strategy
  • Download your top 30 performing reels
  • Repost 5 every single day
  • Creates extra engagement automatically
  • New followers never saw old content
  • People forget what they've seen
